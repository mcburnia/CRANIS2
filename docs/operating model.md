# CRANIS2 Operating Model (v1.1)

---

## 1. Operating Principle

CRANIS2 operates on a **collaborative leadership model with clear accountability**.

### Core Layers

| Layer                     | Owner   | Purpose |
|--------------------------|--------|--------|
| Strategic (What & Why)   | Andi   | Vision, regulation, product direction |
| Technical (How)          | James  | System design, engineering integrity |
| Operational (How Well)   | Tom    | NFRs, performance, cost, runtime quality |

### Cross-Cutting Leadership

- Product ideation → All 5
- Growth & Market → Claire
- Financial Control → Siobhan

---

## 2. Core Decision Rules

- Single accountable owner per domain
- All 5 contribute to product ideation
- Debate → Decide → Commit
- NFR sign-off required before production (Tom)
- Cost decisions require Tom + Siobhan alignment
- Final escalation → Andi

---

## 3. Delivery Flow

### 3.1 Trigger (Product Idea)
Inputs from:
- Regulation (Andi)
- Market (Claire)
- Technology (James)
- Operations/NFR (Tom)
- Financial viability (Siobhan)

→ All 5 contribute

---

### 3.2 Definition
- Product intent → Andi
- Technical approach → James
- NFR & operational targets → Tom
- Cost model → Tom + Siobhan
- Market relevance → Claire

---

### 3.3 Build
- Claude executes
- James governs implementation

---

### 3.4 Validate
- Tom signs off NFRs
- Siobhan validates financial viability

---

### 3.5 Release
- James → Technical readiness
- Tom → Operational readiness

---

### 3.6 Operate

| Area               | Owner   |
|--------------------|--------|
| Runtime operations | Tom    |
| Technical capacity | James  |
| Market feedback    | Claire |
| Direction          | Andi   |
